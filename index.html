RELIABLE & UP TO DATE SMART CONTRACTS VULNERABILITIES DATABASE

Smart contract attacks and security issues can have extreme potential losses. During the last couple of years, we had numerous hacks with hundreds of millions that have been exploited. Considering both the difficulty of upgrading contracts and high exploit risk, smart contract developers must be extremely cautious when importing 3rd party contracts. We aim to help the community gather all the vulnerabilities that we find out in one place.
